Taisho Pond Trail Walk (5 mile RT)

Experience this 2.5-mile point-to-point trail near Matsumoto Shi, Nagano. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 56 min to complete. This is a very popular area for camping, hiking, and road biking, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring.

Okuhido Hotel

This ryokan features free Wi-Fi in all rooms for easy connection, and car park free of charge. Conveniently situated in the Takayama part of Takayama, this property puts you close to attractions and interesting dining options. Don't leave before paying a visit to the famous Hida Folk Village. Rated with 3 stars, this high-quality property provides guests with access to massage, restaurant and hot spring bath on-site.

Hiking Options in Kamikochi

Mount Yakedake

Experience this 4.9-mile out-and-back trail near Matsumoto Shi, Nagano.

Karasawa Cirque

One of major glacial cirques in Japan, located in the Kamikochi region of Azumi, Matsumoto City, in Nagano prefecture.

Isurugi-no-Yu Onsen

“Isurugi-no-Yu” with separate bathing for men and women and both indoor and open-air spa. Guests can enjoy a meal while sitting around the fireplace. The attached Fukuji Fossil Museum exhibits numerous fossils that have been found in the Fukuji area (free entry).

Mount Yakedake

Generally considered a challenging route. This is a very popular area for backpacking, camping, and hiking, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring. The best times to visit this trail are July through October. Mount Yakedake: Shin Nakanoyu Route (焼岳: 新中ノ湯ルート) is a popular route to the summit of Mount Yakedake. Mount Yakedake offers a magnificent views of famous peaks such as the Hotaka Mountain Range. Although it is a suitable trail for climbing beginners, please plan well before coming.
