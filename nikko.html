Nikko

Tobu Tourist Information Center Asakusa

Head to the Tobu Tourist Information Center Asakusa to pick up the Nikko pass. Confirmation number is 9515-1688-4485 for two people. If we have any questions the Tobu Tourist Information center is known for being very helpful in answering any unknowns related to Nikko or how the pass works.
