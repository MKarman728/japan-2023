Tokyo Arrival

WELCOME TO JAPAN!

Look for signs that point you in the direction of the train line you want to take. Follow the signs that will bring you to the train ticket gate.

This is where you would normally buy tickets if you weren't going to use a suica card.

You can also purchase a suica card here.

See below for directions on how to purchase a suica card.

SUICA CARD

  • Select "English" button to begin
  • Select Monorail Suica Purchase
  • You will have the option of "suica" vs "mysuica"

    • (There is no difference other than mysuica will have your name on it.)

  • You will then be prompted to choose how much money you want to put on your card
  • It will ask you to place money into the machine based on the amount selected you would like to add.

    • (Most foreign credit cards do not work with the machine)

  • You now have a suica card!
  • Use the suica card to swipe in and out of ticket gates. Look for signs that say "IC card" to swipe your IC card at.

Hyatt Hotel

  • Take Public Transportation to Hyatt Hotel
  • Confirmation Number: #52296172.
  • Drop off luggage at hotel room.
  • Clean up. Don't forget to bring mask, cloth, and umbrella.

DINNER TIME!
